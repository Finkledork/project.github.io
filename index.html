What is HTML5?

HTML5 (Hyper Text Markup Launguage 5) is a coding launguage commonly used in most websites that you use everyday, including this website!

How did HTML5 come into existence?

In 2008, the first publicly availiable draft was released by the Web Hypertext Applocation Technology Working Group (WHATWG), but in 2014, HTML5 was properly released. HTML5 was compatable with webrowsers such as Microsoft Edge, Google Chrome, Safari, Firefox and Opera. Overtime, people mastered the launguage and created websites that has surpased the limits of what could be achieved through coding.
